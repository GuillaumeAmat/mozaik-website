New Saucelabs widgets for Mozaïk

Say Hi to the new SauceLabs extension for Mozaïk!

This one is from iamfiscus, it comes with 2 widgets at the time I’m writing, one to show the current SauceLabs status from status.saucelabs.com and another one showing a list of your jobs with extra info.

Have a look at the repo’s README for instructions/details.
